Igniting Young Voices Through Film

Published: 18 June 2025

Dear Film Lovers,

We're thrilled to announce that the Mbawula Youth Film Festival is ready to light up screens and spark conversations! This year’s festival is a powerful celebration of local stories, carefully curated to reflect the lived realities, triumphs, and resilience of our communities.

🎬 Locally Curated. Locally Rooted.

We’ve taken time to handpick local films, documentaries, and short films that speak directly to the heartbeat of our people. Whether it’s a coming-of-age drama set in the township, a deeply moving family documentary, or an experimental short tackling youth identity and mental health — each film was selected with purpose.

🔵 Our Stories, Our Power

This festival isn’t just about screening films — it’s about telling our stories, in our voices. We made sure to prioritise narratives that resonate with local audiences, spark necessary dialogue, and empower young storytellers to keep creating.

📍 What To Expect:

  • A diverse lineup of youth-made and youth-focused content
  • Post-screening discussions with filmmakers and community leaders
  • A safe space to engage, learn, and be inspired

💬 Join The Conversation

Let’s come together to celebrate youth creativity and storytelling that speaks directly to who we are and where we come from.
