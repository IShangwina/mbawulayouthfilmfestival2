About the Festival

Mbawula Youth Film Festival is an annual storytelling initiative based in Kimberley, Northern Cape. With additional pop-up screenings in towns like Ritchie and Douglas, the festival brings quality films to local communities across the province.

Focused on nurturing the art of storytelling and encouraging literacy through film, Mbawula showcases powerful documentaries and family-friendly films. The festival explores themes of human rights, social issues, entertainment, and environmental awareness in a non-competitive setting.
